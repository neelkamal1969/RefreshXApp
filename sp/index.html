About RefreshX

Your wellness companion designed to help desk workers prevent strain and fatigue

Why We Created RefreshX

RefreshX is a wellness companion designed to help desk workers prevent strain and fatigue through regular, mindful breaks. The app addresses the common health issues that come with extended computer use - eye strain, back pain, and wrist discomfort - through a thoughtfully designed break scheduling system.

With RefreshX, you can set your work schedule and the app will automatically plan balanced breaks throughout your day. Each break features guided exercises specifically designed for eye relief, back stretching, or wrist care. You'll receive notifications 5 minutes before each scheduled break, helping you transition smoothly from work to wellness moments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about RefreshX

Why aren't my scheduled breaks appearing?

Your break schedule refreshes daily based on your work hours. Pull down to refresh the home screen, and check that your work hours and days are correctly set in your profile. If you've recently changed settings, please restart the app to apply them (though this happens rarely due to network latency).

Do I need to create an account?

When you first enter your email, we'll automatically create an account for you with default settings. After signing in, please customize your profile with details like height and weight for accurate calorie tracking and personalized recommendations.

How does the break scheduler work?

Our intelligent break scheduler evenly distributes your breaks throughout your workday based on your settings. You'll receive a notification 5 minutes before each scheduled break. The scheduler operates on a 24-hour cycle, so work hours must be within the same day.

Can I take unscheduled breaks?

Absolutely! You can start a break anytime by tapping the "Start Break" button on the home screen. All breaks, scheduled or manual, will be tracked in your progress history to help you maintain your wellness streaks.

Are the exercises and articles medically validated?

All our content is curated from reputable health resources including WHO guidelines and peer-reviewed wellness journals. Our team of health professionals reviews each exercise and article to ensure accuracy and safety for general workplace wellness.

What information does RefreshX collect?

We only collect essential data to personalize your experience: email for authentication, name/height/weight for calorie calculations, break settings, routine preferences, and progress history. We never share your personal information with third parties.
